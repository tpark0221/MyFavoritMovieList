Karate Kid

A movie about defeating bulling and taunting. Mr. Miyangi teaches Daniel how to defeat his own enemies and defeat his own inner anger.

Blue Crush

A movie about three ladies living off in the islands of Hawaii. Through failure, there is always a turn of events when one starts to listen closely to the sound of nature. This movie is about conquering one's fears and competing with one's own self.

Despicable Me

A movie about an evil man willing to rule the world with his minions, but along the way meets three sweet, little girls that changes his life. The life of Gru has turned unexpectedly after trying to steal the moon. He finds himself trying to steal back the moon from his arch-nemesis, Chester.

Forest Gump

A movie about a man with a disability who finds himself in all sorts of major life events. Along the way, he finds his way home and discovers the true meaning of home.

Mean Girls

Katie finds herself in all sorts of drama with them, but she ends up finding the important values of being genuine within her friendships at school. This movie is about standing up for oneself, being assertive towards peers, and protecting one's own self-esteem.

Step up 2

A movie about a dancer named, Andie, who gets into deliquency and finds herself in trouble by being friends with the wrong crowd. She finds uniqueness and creativity by meeting all sorts of talented students at a new school. She creates a dance crew filled with unique and quirky dancers at the school. This movie is about seeing the positive in a situation and finding the uniqueness in others.

She's the Man

A movie about a lady in the woman's school soccer team who has a boyfriend and reputation, however, finds herself in a dilemma where the school eliminates the girl's soccer team. Viola is infuriated and decides to take revenge by pretending to be her brother and tries to scam the school just so she can play in the men's soccer team. She gets revenge on her boyfriend and ends up making it on the men's soccer team. This movie is about fighting for your ambitions and never giving up on your passions.

Divergent

A movie about a world with factions. The factions are; Dauntless, Amity, Erudite, Abnegation, and Candor. A girl who ends up being a divergent, must save her world from destruction and war. She must protect her family's hidden secrets and hide her identity as a Divergent.

Coco

A movie about a boy who dreams of becoming a musician. While his family prepares for the special holiday, Day of the Dead, Miguel wants to prove that music is part of his life. He finds himself in the underworld and discovers his long lost grandfather. However, time is running out in which Miguel's great grandmother, Coco, must remember her son. Coco's son helps Miguel get back to the living world. Miguel helps revive his great grandfathers legacy and helps Coco to remember who she is and who her family is.

Princess Diaries

A movie about a teenage girl who finds out she is a princess from her grandmother. Her grandmother helps her discover how to act, behave, and speak like a princess. Mia, the princess, gets the help from her true friends to become an elegent princess. She uses her high status to help children and rebuild her own self.
